Engineer Game is a personalized method designed achieve your life goals by finding a job that aligns with your values and aspirations

Session Content
Pre-work
  • Identify your life goals and how your career fits into them.
  • Define your career path and plan your next steps.
  • Learn effective habits to improve your productivity and performance.
Session 1
  • Resume preparation and tuning: I'll help you create a resume that stands out from the crowd. I'll help you highlight your strengths, accomplishments, and relevant experience, and I'll make sure your resume is easy to read and visually appealing.
  • Adjusting experience and skills: I ll work with you to make sure your resume accurately reflects your experience and skills. I'll help you identify the most relevant experiences and skills to include on your resume, and I'll make sure they're presented in a way that highlights your strengths.
  • LinkedIn profile: Your LinkedIn profile is an important tool for job hunting. I'll help you create a profile that showcases your skills and experience, and that makes you stand out to recruiters and hiring managers.
  • VISA support: If you need help with visa applications or other immigration-related issues, we're here to help. I'll provide guidance and support to help you navigate the process and make sure you have all the information you need to succeed.
Session 2
  • Companies applications: I'll help you identify the companies you want to work for and create a plan for applying to them. I'll also provide guidance on how to research companies and find job listings that match your skills and experience.
  • Prioritize interviews: Once you start receiving interview invitations, I'll help you prioritize them and decide which ones to accept. I'll help you weigh the pros and cons of each opportunity and make informed decisions about your job search.
  • Prepare recruiter questions: I'll help you prepare for interviews by providing a list of common recruiter questions and strategies for answering them. I'll help you develop your own list of questions to ask recruiters during interviews, so you can get the information you need to make informed decisions about job offers.
Session 3
  • Prepare technical interviews: I'll help you prepare for technical interviews by reviewing data structures and algorithms, and by providing tips and strategies for solving common coding problems. I'll also provide mock interviews to help you get comfortable with the interview process and build your confidence.
Session 4
  • Prepare soft skills questions: I'll help you prepare for Soft skills interviews by providing a list of common questions and strategies for answering them. We'll also provide tips on how to showcase your interpersonal skills and demonstrate your ability to work well with others.
Session 5
  • On-site interviews. I will help you research the company and the interviewers, develop strategies for handling different types of interview questions, and provide tips for staying calm and confident during the interview.
Session 6
  • Negotiation and evaluation: I'll help you negotiate and evaluate job offers to ensure you get the best compensation package possible. I'll discuss strategies for handling multiple offers and prepare a counteroffer if needed.
  • Benefits and equity options: It's important to evaluate benefits and equity options when considering a job offer. I'll discuss what to look for and how to evaluate these options to make the best decision.
Session 7
  • Preparing for the job: I'll work with you to make sure you're ready for your new job, whether it's in-person or remote. I'll help you manage your expectations and prepare for the transition.
  • Setting up your office: If you're working from home, I'll help you set up a productive workspace that meets your needs. I'll discuss best practices for working from home and maintaining work-life balance.
Session 8
  • Leadership and mindset: I'll help you develop the leadership skills and mindset needed to succeed in your new role. I'll discuss strategies for effective communication, time management, and prioritization.
  • Teamwork and meetings: Working effectively with others is key to success in any job. I'll discuss best practices for teamwork and how to make the most of meetings.
  • Promotions and advancement: I'll discuss strategies for advancing your career, including how to ask for a promotion, build your network, and develop new skills.
